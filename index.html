Activity Instructions

  1. Divide the budgeted costs of January into activity cost pools and label the cost drivers.

  2. Compare and contrast your activity cost pools to other students.

01.

Activity Cost Pools

Hi , divide the budgeted costs by selecting each cost item in the drop down menu. Type a label that identifies a cost driver for assigning each pool of costs to products.

Up to eight cost pools can be used for this activity, but you need not use all eight. To remove a cost item from a cost pool you have selected, click the X next to item. Each cost must be added in a pool and the cost driver labeled before you can submit the activity.
